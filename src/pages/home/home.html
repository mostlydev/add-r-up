Sample Rules
  • Table buys in for $20, players are issued 550 in chips (3x 100, 6x 25, & 10x 10). Blinds start at 10/20.
  • Players are allowed unlimited re-buys until "last hand" for $20 and 550 in chips. You cannot re-buy if you haven't been completely knocked out!
  • Timer starts at 45 minutes, after which blinds double to 20/40.
  • 45 minutes later, blinds increase again to 30/60, and the timer is decreased to 30 minutes.
  • Deal is allowed to go around the table one more, independent of time. This is the "last hand" for re-buys.
  • At the end of the "last hand", any player can top-up for $20 and receive 450 in chips.
  • A player knocked out during the "last hand" can re-buy and top-up for $40, and get a total of 1,000 in chips.
  • Blinds continue to increase every 30 minutes: 30/60, 40/80, 50/100, with no more re-buys, until someone wins or a deal is made.
  • Blinds can optionally be frozen at 50/100 until there are three players left, 100/200 for the last three, and 200/400 for the last two.
